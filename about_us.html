About Easy Bank

Easy Bank is a digital bank that is making banking easier for everyone. It was founded in 2023 by a group of experienced bankers and technologists who were frustrated with the traditional banking system. They wanted to create a bank that was simple, easy to use, and transparent.Easy Bank offers a wide range of banking products and services, including checking and savings accounts, loans, credit cards, and investments. All of its products and services are designed to be easy to understand and use. Easy Bank is also committed to providing its customers with excellent customer service. It offers 24/7 customer support by phone, chat, and email. Easy Bank also has a team of financial advisors who are available to help customers with their financial planning needs.
